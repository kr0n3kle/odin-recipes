Drunken Noodles

Description

This is amazing, drunk or not! A very fulfilling meal. Go with all organic ingredients for an unmatchable experience.

Ingredients

  • Sweet soy sauce (kecap manis)
  • Fish sauce
  • Rice vinegar
  • Garlic
  • hot peppers like thai chilies
  • Organic refinded coconut oil
  • Onion
  • Protein of your choice
  • Bell pepper
  • Wide rice noodles
  • Thai basil (or regular basil)
  • Lime

Steps

  • To make sauce: To make the sauce, simply stir together 1/4 cup sweet soy sauce, 1/4 cup fish sauce and 2 teaspoons rice vinegar.
  • Stir together the fish sauce, sweet soy sauce and vinegar.
  • Chop the garlic and 3 of the chiles together. Chop the other chile, and set aside.
  • Cook noodles approximately 7 minutes if using organic pad thai noodels
  • Heat a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat.
  • Add the oil, the garlic/chile mixture and the onion. Cook, stirring constantly, until the garlic releases its fragrance, about 30 seconds.
  • Add the protein and a splash of the sauce. Cook 5 minutes.
  • Drain the noodles and add them with the bell peppers to the pan. Increase the heat to high, and add the sauce.
  • Cook, tossing everything together and separating the noodles, until all ingredients are coated with the sauce and it thickens slightly, about 8-10 minutes.
  • Cook until all noodles are coated with sauce and it thickens, about 8-10 minutes.
  • Add basil, some lime juice and the additional chile.
  • Enjoy!
